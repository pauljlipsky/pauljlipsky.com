The Skills

Each one is a standalone markdown file. Download what you need, skip what you don't.

Brain Dump

Catches chaotic streams of thought and organizes them into projects, tasks, connections, and actionable next steps. Just dump everything on your mind — it turns the mess into a clean system.

Email Setup

One-time setup that interviews you about your email patterns, business context, and reply style, then generates a complete knowledge base that powers automated inbox triage.

Email Triage

Recurring inbox processor that classifies emails, researches new senders, drafts replies in your voice, tracks conversations, and delivers a clean triage report. Runs on demand or on a schedule.

Take a Step Back

Mid-conversation reflection that zooms out from the details. Performs gap analysis, bias checks, and contextual alignment to make sure you're solving the right problem the right way.

NotebookLM Controller

Browser automation skill that controls Google NotebookLM — open notebooks, add sources, ask questions, and generate Studio outputs like podcasts, infographics, slide decks, and more.

Landing Page Generator

Creates premium 3D animated HTML landing pages with GSAP ScrollTrigger effects, mouse parallax, and a polished brand system — all from a simple text prompt.

Last 30 Days Research

Deep multi-source research across Reddit, Hacker News, the web, and X/Twitter. Synthesizes what people are actually saying about any topic into an actionable briefing with real citations.
